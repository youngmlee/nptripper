Welcome to National Park Tripper - the road trip planner based around the amazing national parks of the United States!

1. Click the map markers to see more information about a national park. (Green star markers indicate a top 10 yearly most-visited park)
2. Enter your origin,destination, and any waypoints you would like to stop by on your road trip to preview your route!
3. (Optional Adventure Checkpoints) - Choose strategic locations along your route to search for national parks to generate a preview!
